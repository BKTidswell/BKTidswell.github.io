Neural Network Evolution

 For my final project in my neural modeling class I created an evolutionary neural network population. The goal of this project was to determine whether changing the task they had to solve increased modualrity in a popualtion. Neural networks were randomly created for the initial population. They were evaluated on their ability to solve the XOR problem. Based on how well they managed to approximate XOR they then had offspring. In the experimental groups the task was switched from XOR to NOT-XOR a number of times. While populations were able to re-optimize to the new task after the switch there was no increase in modualrity seen with more switches between XOR and NOT-XOR.
